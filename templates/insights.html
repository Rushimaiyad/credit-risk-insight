How It Works

Credit Risk Insight uses a Logistic Regression machine learning model trained on historical lending data. The system evaluates several financial and demographic factors to estimate the statistical probability of a borrower missing consecutive payments (defaulting).

Credit Score Influence

A higher credit score represents a proven history of managing debt, heavily decreasing the mathematical probability of default.

High Impact

Income-to-Loan Ratio

The relationship between Annual Income and Loan Amount directly determines the borrower's capacity to repay.

Significant Impact

Employment Type & Tenure

Salaried workers with a longer employment history typically exhibit lower variance in income, reducing risk.

Moderate Impact

Existing Credit Lines

A high number of existing credit lines increases overall debt burden, leading to an increased probability of default.

Moderate Impact
